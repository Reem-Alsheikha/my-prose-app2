# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment
- Discrimination
- Personal attacks

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.
